  • Tried everything: CIO, sleep sacks, white noise

Referral Partner Scripts

🤝 These local professionals see exhausted parents every single week. A simple introduction can unlock a steady stream of warm referrals. Click any script to copy it.

👶 Pediatricians & Family Doctors

They hear sleep complaints at nearly every well-child visit. A small referral card at the front desk or a 5-min conversation with the office manager is all it takes.

📧 Email / Drop-In Script
Hi Dr. [Name], I'm Kerri Young, a Certified Pediatric Sleep Consultant in the area. I work with families of newborns through toddlers who are struggling with sleep. I'd love to leave a few referral cards with your office — I know sleep issues come up all the time! My approach is evidence-based and gentle. Happy to chat if you'd like to know more. — Kerri, blossomingslumber.com

🤰 Postpartum Doulas & Midwives

They support families in the exact window when sleep problems begin. Position yourself as the natural "next step" after their postpartum care ends.

💬 DM / Text Script
Hey [Name]! I'm Kerri, a certified pediatric sleep consultant. I think our work is really complementary — you support families through birth and early weeks, and I help when the sleep challenges really set in. Would you be open to a quick 15-min call? I'd love to swap referrals and see if we can serve our families better together!

🍼 Lactation Consultants & IBCLCs

Many nursing parents are up multiple times a night and aren't sure what's hunger vs. habit. You solve the habit side — they'll appreciate having someone to refer to.

📧 Email Script
Hi [Name], I'm Kerri Young, a Certified Pediatric Sleep Consultant. I work with a lot of nursing moms who want to reduce night wakings without fully night weaning — I always defer feeding decisions to an IBCLC! I'd love to explore a referral relationship. Many of my clients need your help, and vice versa. My site is blossomingslumber.com — would love to connect!

🧘 Mommy & Me Classes / Baby Gyms

Parents at these classes are actively investing in their child's development and are warm to expert help. Ask if you can leave flyers or do a free 10-min "sleep tip" talk.

💬 In-Person Pitch
Hi! I'm Kerri, a pediatric sleep consultant based locally. I was wondering if I could leave a few of my cards here or possibly do a short free sleep tips session for your families one day? I work with parents of newborns through toddlers — sleep is one of the biggest struggles parents have and I'd love to help your community!
